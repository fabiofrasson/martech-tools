This Privacy Policy describes how Essential MarTech Tools (referred to as "We", "Our") collects, uses, and protects the information of users who use our free online tools ("Service").

1. Types of Data Collected

Our Service focuses on tools and does not require registration. Data collection is essentially passive:

1.1 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include:

  • Internet Protocol address (IP address).
  • Browser type and version.
  • Pages of our Service visited.
  • Time spent on those pages.

1.2 User-Provided Data (Not Stored)

Information entered by the user in the tools (e.g., Cookie Names in the Generator or Regular Expressions in the Validator) is **processed client-side (your browser)** and is **NOT** stored on our servers, databases, or logs.

2. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. You may instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly.

3. Third-Party Services (Google AdSense and Analytics)

3.1 Google AdSense (Advertising)

The DART cookie enables Google to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

Recommendation: Review the Google Advertising Policy.
